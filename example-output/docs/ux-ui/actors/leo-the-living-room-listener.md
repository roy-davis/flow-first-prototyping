# Leo the Living Room Listener - Audiobook listener

> File: docs/ux-ui/actors/leo-the-living-room-listener.md
> Actor type: Human

**Composite summary:** Leo listens from the couch and expects the TV to behave like a dedicated long-form audio player, not a generic file browser. He wants the app to open on the exact book and millisecond he left, keep controls legible from across the room, and make source failures recoverable without losing his place.
